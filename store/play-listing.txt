## Full description EN
WebMMD Suite is a mobile studio for MMD (MikuMikuDance) content on your phone.

Load a model folder or ZIP with PMX/PMD, textures, and optional VMD motion files. Preview in 3D, play animations, adjust morphs and bones, use the timeline, and apply visual effects.

Features:
• Import PMX/PMD + textures (PNG, TGA, BMP) from ZIP or folder
• VMD motion playback with optional camera motion
• Timeline with keyframes and animation templates
• Morph and bone controls
• MMD physics (when supported on device)
• Portrait fullscreen interface optimized for phones

User-provided content only — you supply your own models and motions. The app does not include copyrighted characters or music.

Requirements: Android 6.0+, OpenGL ES 3.0 recommended for best performance.

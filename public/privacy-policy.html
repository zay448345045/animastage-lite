WebMMD Suite — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

WebMMD Suite (“the app”) is a local MMD model viewer and animation tool. This policy describes how the app handles information.

Summary

  • We do not collect, sell, or share personal data.
  • Models and files you load stay on your device unless you export or share them yourself.
  • The app requires Internet permission for the embedded WebView runtime only; it does not upload your models to our servers.

Information we do not collect

The app does not require an account and does not collect:

  • name, email, or contact details;
  • location;
  • contacts or photos (except files you explicitly pick through the system file picker);
  • analytics or advertising identifiers (unless added in a future version — this policy will be updated).

Files you open in the app

PMX/PMD/VMD/ZIP files you select are processed locally in memory and temporary browser storage on your device. We do not receive copies of those files.

Third-party content

You are responsible for rights to models, motions, music, and textures you load. The app is a tool only and does not provide copyrighted MMD content.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change when we do. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
